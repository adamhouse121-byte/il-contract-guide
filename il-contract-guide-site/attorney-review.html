Attorney Review in Illinois — 5 Business Days, Explained

What the window is for, how modifications work, when you can cancel, and how to avoid timeline mistakes.

What this clause is trying to do

Give each side’s attorney a brief window after acceptance to review the contract, propose changes, or cancel.

What it says (plain English)

  • Once everyone signs, a clock starts for attorney review (commonly 5 business days from acceptance; check your contract).
  • During that time, an attorney may approve, propose modifications, or disapprove/cancel per the form.
  • If modifications are proposed, the other side can accept, reject, or counter. If you can’t agree within the window (or a written extension), either side may cancel.

How the clock is counted

  • Business days usually mean Monday–Friday, excluding standard holidays. Confirm your form.
  • Day 1 is typically the first business day after acceptance.
  • If the last day falls on a weekend/holiday, many forms push to the next business day.

Three common paths

  1. Approve & move forward.
  2. Propose modifications. Agree within the window (or a written extension) and continue.
  3. Disapprove/cancel. Only as permitted by your form and within the window.

Buyer tips

  • Engage an attorney early so they’re ready on Day 1.
  • Track earnest-money and inspection clocks; these often overlap.
  • Ask your attorney to flag HOA/condo docs and special assessments early.

Seller tips

  • Have disclosures and HOA docs ready to speed review.
  • If you need a rent‑back, draft it cleanly now.
  • Decide in advance which changes you can accept.

What if you miss the window?

If neither side acts within time, the contingency may lapse as written. Don’t assume—get written clarity from your attorney.

Helpful “write‑it‑in” examples (run by your attorney)

  • Extension: Attorney review period extended by 2 business days to [date]. All other terms unchanged.
  • Rent‑back: Seller to remain in possession until [date/time]. Use & Occupancy at $[amount]/day. Security deposit $[amount], refundable at keys delivered and property in substantially same condition.
  • Clarify escrow timing: Earnest money due within 2 business days of acceptance to [escrowee], check or wire.

FAQ

Does attorney review allow cancel for any reason? Only as permitted by your contract and within the window.

Can we negotiate new items? Yes—if both sides agree in writing during the window.

Do weekends count? Typically no, but confirm your form’s rules.
