If the Appraisal is Low — Options & Scripts

What to do if value comes in below purchase price.

Options

  1. Renegotiate price or credits.
  2. Bring cash gap if that fits your plan.
  3. Cancel if your financing/appraisal contingency allows and you act on time.

Scripts

  • Price renegotiation: “Appraisal returned at $X. Buyer requests new purchase price of $X (attached report page).”
  • Credit: “In lieu of price reduction, buyer requests $X closing credit.”

Tip

Share the appraisal page(s) your lender allows; confirm contract rules about attaching reports.
