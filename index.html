1) Parties

Names of buyer(s)/seller(s). Only listed parties are bound. Ensure names & signing capacity are correct.

2) The Real Estate

Address, legal/PINs, parking/storage. Confirms what you’re buying.

3) Price, Earnest, Credits

Purchase price, earnest timing, credits, balance due.

4) Seller Contribution to Buyer’s Brokerage Compensation

States if seller will pay a %/$ toward buyer’s brokerage fee—or not. Impacts buyer cash unless negotiated.
