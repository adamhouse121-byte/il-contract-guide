Condo/HOA Documents — What to Look For

Buyers receive governing documents, budgets, assessments, and key disclosures (e.g., condo 22.1).

Red Flags

  • Pending special assessments
  • Low reserves
  • Major litigation

Tip

Many forms allow a short review/cancel window if documents are unacceptable—watch the date.
