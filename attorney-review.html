Attorney Review in Illinois — 5 Business Days, Explained

What the window is for, how modifications work, when you can cancel, and how to avoid timeline mistakes.

What this clause is trying to do

Give each side’s attorney a brief window after acceptance to review the contract, propose changes, or cancel.

What it says (plain English)

  • Clock starts at acceptance (commonly 5 business days—confirm your form).
  • Attorney may approve, propose modifications, or disapprove/cancel.
  • If no agreement within the window or an agreed extension, either side may cancel per the form.

Tips

  • Day 1 is usually the first business day after acceptance.
  • Get extensions in writing if needed.
