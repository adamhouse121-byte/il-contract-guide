Home‑Sale Contingency & Kick‑Out — How it Works

Your purchase depends on selling your current home; the seller can keep marketing under a kick‑out.

Kick‑Out Mechanics

Seller may accept another offer and give you written notice. You usually have a short time to waive your contingency (often with extra earnest money) or the contract can end.

Buyer Tips

  • Know your waiver deadline and additional earnest requirement.
  • Have lender and attorney ready to pivot quickly.

Seller Tips

  • Follow the notice steps in your form exactly.
  • Keep marketing to maintain leverage.
