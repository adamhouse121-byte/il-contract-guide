Rent‑Back (Use & Occupancy) — Template & Checklist

If seller needs to stay after closing, use a short addendum to outline terms.

Template (run by your attorney)

  • Use & Occupancy from closing to [date/time] at $[amount]/day.
  • Deposit $[amount] held by escrowee; refundable if property returned in substantially same condition.
  • Seller maintains utilities/insurance until surrender.
  • Keys/garage remotes transferred at [handoff time].

Checklist

  • Exact move‑out date/time
  • Daily rate & deposit
  • Damage/cleaning expectations
  • Access for buyer (walk‑through)
