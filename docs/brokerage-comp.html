Seller Contribution to Buyer’s Brokerage — Explained

A selection where the seller can pay a stated %/$ toward the buyer’s brokerage fee—or not.

Why it matters

It changes the buyer’s cash‑to‑close unless adjusted in price or credit. Clarity up front prevents disputes at closing.

Tips

  • State the exact % or $ and whether it’s paid at closing through title.
  • If seller won’t contribute, buyer can negotiate price/credit or budget for the fee.
