Directions to Escrowee & Notices — Plain English

How earnest money is handled and how to give valid contract notices.

Directions to Escrowee (Earnest Money)

  • Escrowee holds funds and releases per contract or mutual direction.
  • If there’s a dispute, escrowee may hold or interplead (ask court).

Notices — Delivery & Timing

  • Use the delivery methods listed in the contract (personal, mail, fax, email, overnight).
  • Send to the names/addresses/emails written in the contract and copy your attorney.
  • Deadlines landing on weekends/holidays usually roll to the next business day—confirm your form.

Template (email)

Subject: Notice under Paragraph [#] — [Property Address]

Per the Contract dated [Acceptance Date], Buyer hereby provides notice of [e.g., disapproval / inspection request / financing denial].

Signed,
[Buyer/Agent Name]
[Contact Info]
