Title & Survey — What “Clean” Means

Title commitment shows ownership and exceptions. A proper survey shows boundaries and encroachments.

Title

  • Unacceptable exceptions must be removed or insured over.
  • Make sure payoff letters and releases are ordered early.

Survey

  • Real boundary survey (not a fast mortgage inspection) is best for fences, sheds, driveways.
  • Attorney can advise which survey type is required.
