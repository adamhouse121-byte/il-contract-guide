If the Appraisal is Low — Options & Scripts

When value comes in below price, you can renegotiate, bridge the gap, or cancel if your contingency allows.

Options

  1. Price reduction
  2. Closing credit to offset costs
  3. Buyer brings cash gap
  4. Cancel if allowed and on time

Scripts

  • Price: “Appraisal returned at $X. Buyer requests new price of $X. See attached page.”
  • Credit: “In lieu of price reduction, Buyer requests a $X closing credit.”
