Title & Survey — What “Clean” Means

Title commitment with extended coverage and a proper survey help confirm you’re getting what you think you’re getting.

Title Commitment

  • Shows who owns the property and what liens/easements exist.
  • Unacceptable exceptions must be cured or insured over.

Survey

  • Real boundary survey (not a quick mortgage inspection) shows fences, sheds, driveways, and encroachments.
  • Ask your attorney which type of survey is required for your property type.
