Fixtures & Personal Property — What Stays With the Home

If it’s attached or built‑in, assume it stays unless excluded. Freestanding items don’t—unless you list them.

Typical fixtures (stay)

  • Built‑in appliances and microwave hoods
  • Light fixtures, ceiling fans, switch plates
  • TV wall‑mount brackets (TVs may go)
  • Wired smart devices (thermostat, doorbell/cameras, hubs)
  • Garage door openers & remotes

Gray areas

  • Window treatments: curtains vs rods
  • EV chargers: hardwired vs plug‑in
  • Rented items (water softener, propane tanks)

Write‑it‑in examples

  • Include: “Existing smart thermostat and wired doorbell to remain; seller to transfer app credentials at closing.”
  • Exclude: “All televisions excluded; wall‑mount brackets and hardware to remain.”
