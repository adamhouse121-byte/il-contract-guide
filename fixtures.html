Fixtures & Personal Property — What Stays With the Home?

If it’s attached or built‑in, assume it stays unless excluded. Freestanding items don’t—unless you write them in.

What stays (typical)

  • Built‑in appliances
  • TV wall‑mount brackets
  • Smart thermostat / wired hubs
  • Light fixtures, ceiling fans, curtain rods

Gray areas

  • Window treatments: curtains vs rods
  • Smart doorbells/cameras/locks
  • EV chargers: hardwired vs plug‑in
  • Rented items (softener/propane)

Write‑it‑in examples

  • Televisions excluded; wall‑mount brackets to remain.
  • Existing smart thermostat and doorbell to remain; seller to transfer app credentials at closing.
