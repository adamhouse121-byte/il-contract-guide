Contact

Have a contract question or want your key dates translated? Send a note here. This form can email you directly (via Formspree) or fall back to your email app.

Tip: To enable direct email delivery, replace REPLACE_ME in the form action with your Formspree endpoint.

How to enable the form (1 minute)

  1. Create a free Formspree form and copy your endpoint (looks like /f/xyzabcde).
  2. In GitHub, open docs/contact.html, click the pencil, and replace REPLACE_ME in the form action.
  3. Commit changes. Test the form — it should redirect to the thank-you page.
