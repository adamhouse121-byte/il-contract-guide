Seller Contribution to Buyer’s Brokerage Compensation — Explained

A new selection where the seller can agree to pay a stated % or $ toward the buyer’s brokerage fee — or decline to pay it.

Why this matters

It changes the buyer’s out‑of‑pocket unless otherwise negotiated in price/credits.

Buyer Tips

  • If seller won’t contribute, budget for your brokerage fee or negotiate elsewhere (price/credit).

Seller Tips

  • Decide early and state it clearly to avoid disputes.
