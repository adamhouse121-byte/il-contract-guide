Taxes & Prorations — How the Money is Calculated

How property taxes, assessments, and HOA items are split at closing.

Standard Prorations

Taxes and certain charges are prorated as of closing so each side pays their share for the period they own the home.

Improved‑But‑Not‑Fully‑Taxed

If the property was improved but not fully taxed for a full year, contracts often require an escrow (e.g., a percentage of price) held until the actual bill is known and the parties settle up.

Tips

  • Ask title/attorney for the estimated tax proration on your settlement statement.
  • Confirm HOA special assessments and whether they’re paid or credited.
